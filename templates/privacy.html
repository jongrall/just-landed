Protecting Your Privacy

  • The security of your personal information is important to us. Your SimplyListed account information is protected by a password for your privacy and security. You need to work to protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer by signing off once you have finished using a shared computer.
  • When you enter sensitive information SimplyListed encrypts that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). SimplyListed follows generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once SimplyListed receives it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, while SimplyListed strives to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, SimplyListed cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on SimplyListed's website, you can email SimplyListed at .
  • SimplyListed will not sell or disclose your name or email address to third parties without your explicit consent, except as specified in this privacy policy.
  • SimplyListed does not store credit card information. Credit card transactions are transmitted to a financial gateway, and we endeavor to protect the security of your payment information during transmission by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology.
  • Your first name and last initial, username, user ID or alias is displayed throughout SimplyListed (and so available to the public) and is connected to all of your SimplyListed activity. Other people can see your items for sale, feedback, ratings and associated comments.

Data We Collect

  • SimplyListed collects personal information when you register to use SimplyListed services or post content on SimplyListed. At registration you are asked for your first name, last name, email address, and to provide a password.
  • If you would like to sell on SimplyListed, SimplyListed will ask for your phone number, and may ask for your billing address and credit card information.
  • We may collect your phone number for account authentication purposes, and may transmit it to a third party service for tele-robotic verification.
  • When you list an item for sale on SimplyListed using our mobile applications, we record images that you upload to us, their associated metadata, and the location where the item was posted. Your location data is only used to figure out the approximate neighborhood and city in which to post your item for sale, or anonymously and in aggregate for us to understand usage patterns. Your specific address or location will never be shared with any other users of SimplyListed unless you explicitly send another user this information.
  • When you list an item for sale on SimplyListed, the images you take and the descriptions you provide may be sent to servers operated by third parties or shown to individuals being paid by SimplyListed for the purposes of identifying the items you are selling. No personally identifiable information is sent along with these images and descriptions.
  • We may collect personal information if you provide it in feedback or comments, post it on our forums, or in the description of an item you are selling, or if you contact us directly. Please do not post any personal information on SimplyListed that you expect to keep private.
  • SimplyListed automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookies, and the pages you request.
  • Through the use of our mobile applications, SimplyListed automatically receives and records usage information and stores this information on servers operated by Mixpanel Inc. This data includes the model of any mobile device used to access the service, the version number of the software on your mobile device, and your devices unique identifier (if applicable). This data is only used in the aggregate.
  • SimplyListed uses information for the following general purposes: products and services provisioning, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, research, and anonymous reporting. IP addresses are automatically reported by your browser each time you view a web page, or each time your mobile device makes a request to the SimplyListed API. Your IP address may be stored in SimplyListed's user registration databases when you register with SimplyListed. IP addresses may be used for various research purposes.
  • All postings are stored in our database, even after “deletion,” and may be archived elsewhere.
  • Our web logs and other records are stored indefinitely.
  • Search engines and other sites not affiliated with SimplyListed including google.com, bing.com, and others archive or otherwise make available SimplyListed postings.

Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. You can configure your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. If you reject all cookies, you will not be able to use SimplyListed products or services that require you to “sign in,” and you may not be able to take full advantage of the SimplyListed service. SimplyListed uses its own cookies for a number of purposes, including to: Access your information when you “sign in”; keep track of preferences you specify while you are using SimplyListed's services; display the most appropriate advertising banners, based on your interests and activity on SimplyListed; estimate and report SimplyListed's total audience size and traffic; conduct research to improve SimplyListed's content and services.

Tracking Technologies

We employ a software technology called Clear Gifs/Web Beacons, that help us better manage content on our site by informing us what content is effective. Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of Web users. In contrast to cookies, which are stored on a user's computer hard drive, clear gifs are embedded invisibly on Web pages. The use of cookies by our third party tracking partners is not covered by our privacy statement. We do not have access or control over these cookies. Our partners use session ID cookies to support our site functionality such as web analytics reporting.
